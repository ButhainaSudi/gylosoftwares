About GyLo

Practical software engineering for growing companies.

GyLo Softwares builds custom software, AI automation systems, and digital platforms for businesses that need better operations, sales workflows, reporting, and customer service.

We build systems that support real work.

About the Founder

A note from Buthaina Isihaka Sudi, Co-Founder and CEO.

I am Buthaina Isihaka Sudi, Co-Founder and Chief Executive Officer of GyLo Softwares Company Limited. My work brings together software engineering, data science, artificial intelligence, and enterprise systems development to help organizations build practical, scalable, and business-driven digital solutions.

I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, majoring in Software Engineering, from Universiti Malaysia Sabah, and I am currently pursuing a Master's Degree in Data Science at the University of Dar es Salaam. This background helps me approach technology not only as a developer, but as a problem-solver focused on data, automation, system design, and measurable business value.

Professionally, I have worked as a Backend Engineer with production systems, APIs, databases, bug resolution, system improvements, and client-driven change requests. That experience shapes how GyLo Softwares delivers: clean architecture, strong backend foundations, user-friendly interfaces, and systems built to support business growth.

My mission is to help Tanzanian businesses and institutions move beyond basic websites and adopt digital systems that improve operations, attract customers, automate workflows, and support confident decision-making.
